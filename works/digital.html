Digital Fabrication
Instructor: Alphonso Peluso

The objective of the class is to become familiar with the use of a CNC machine in order to create molds that will in turn be used to lay carbon fiber onto. It eventually progresses to working on a large carbon fiber panel that is added to panels created the previous three semesters.
